# Single Source of Truth (SSOT)

## What is SSOT?

A Single Source of Truth is the official location where a business metric is defined and maintained.

Every analyst uses the same definition.

---

## Why Do We Need SSOT?

Without SSOT:

- Different dashboards show different values.
- Teams disagree on KPIs.
- Decision-making becomes unreliable.

With SSOT:

- Consistent metrics.
- Trusted dashboards.
- Better business decisions.

---

## Real Business Example

Instead of storing metric definitions in Google Sheets, Google built a centralized version-controlled table.

Benefits:

- Automatic dashboard updates.
- Approval workflow.
- Reduced manual effort.
- Better consistency.

---

## Key Takeaways

- One metric.
- One definition.
- One trusted source.

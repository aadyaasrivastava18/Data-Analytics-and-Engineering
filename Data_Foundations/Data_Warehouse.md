# Data Warehouse

## What is a Data Warehouse?

A Data Warehouse stores cleaned, validated, and business-ready data optimised for reporting and analytics.

---

## Purpose

- Trusted reporting.
- Fast analytical queries.
- Consistent KPIs.
- Business-ready datasets.

---

## Characteristics

- Structured data.
- Optimised for analytics.
- Centralised business logic.
- High query performance.

---

## Why Use a Data Warehouse?

A warehouse ensures that every team uses the same definitions of business metrics.

---

## Business Example

Google dashboards are built using warehouse data instead of raw Data Lake data.

---

## Key Takeaways

- Warehouse = Trusted Business Data.
- Built for dashboards.
- Supports decision-making.

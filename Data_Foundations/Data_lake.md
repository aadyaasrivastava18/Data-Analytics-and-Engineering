# Data Lake

## What is a Data Lake?

A Data Lake stores raw historical data from multiple sources in its original format.

It can contain:

- Structured data
- Semi-structured data
- Unstructured data

---

## Purpose

- Store raw data.
- Preserve historical records.
- Support future business use cases.
- Enable flexible analytics.

---

## Characteristics

- Large-scale storage.
- Raw data.
- Multiple data formats.
- Historical repository.

---

## Why Not Analyze Directly on a Data Lake?

- Data is not business-ready.
- Business logic is not standardized.
- Analysts repeat transformations.
- Dashboards become inconsistent.

---

## Business Example

Google stores raw Ads logs inside a Data Lake before transforming them through pipelines into business-ready datasets.

---

## Key Takeaways

- Data Lake stores raw historical data.
- It is not designed for dashboards.
- It supports future analytical needs.
